Overview

The Manufacturing Data Analytics (MDA) Lab is a research group in the Department of Industrial Engineering at Pusan National University, led by Prof. Kwangyeol Ryu. Our lab conducts cutting-edge research in Smart Manufacturing Systems and Data-Driven Manufacturing Innovation.


We focus on collecting, analyzing, and leveraging diverse manufacturing data to achieve intelligent and optimized production systems. By integrating key Industry 4.0 technologies — including Digital Twin, AI/ML, IoT, and AR/VR — into manufacturing environments, we actively pursue research toward realizing next-generation Smart Factories.
